About me

Hi! I am Michi, a UX designer who is all about creating intuitive, inclusive, and human-centered experiences.

I am currently studying UX Design and constantly expanding my skills—especially when it comes to accessibility, which is a big focus for me right now. I want to make sure the things I design work for everyone, not just the average user.

I am super curious by nature, and I love digging into what people need, testing ideas, and finding simple, thoughtful solutions. Whether it is wireframes, user flows, or high-fidelity prototypes, I try to design with empathy and intention. With a strong foundation in user research and interaction design, I focus on crafting interfaces that are both visually appealing and highly functional.

Besides design, one of my biggest passions is dancing—mainly hip hop and house. For me, dance is about freedom, expression, and connecting with people.

Right now, I am exploring HTML and CSS to get a better understanding of frontend development, working on app prototypes, and learning as much as I can about accessible, responsible design. Always open to new ideas, challenges, and collaborations.

Let's build things that not only work—but feel good to use.
